Information about- lessons

We do lessons every friday, saturday and sunday between 9:30am to 5:00pm. If you are looking to do regular entry it costs $20 for adults and $15 for kids. Our opening and closing times are from 9:30am to 7:00pm everyday of the week. To book a snowboarding or ski lesson in advance please contact the front desk and if you have someone in mind to be instructed by, please tell us their name and we will do our best to accomadate. Lessons from an instructor cost $50 an hour and if you do a 3 hour lesson we give a 15 percent discount. /p>

Vision and Mission- Statement

Our vision is to expand our resort to other parts of the country so everyone can have a taste of the snowbaording and skiers adventure. We want to build a community of snowboarders and skiers that have a second place to call home. Our business has been around for 30 years and hopefully 30 more. Our mission is to help each rider that comes through our door to feel accomplished when they leave. We want everyone to have a great overall experience with us and to come back again.
