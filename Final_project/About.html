About Judahs GripArt!

During Covid-19 many skate companies weren't producing colorful gripart or didn't have many graphic grip to choose from. I love having colorful grip rather than just plaine black griptape, so I bought markers online and started doing art on regular griptape. This allowed me to make my skateboard a canvas and start selling my art to other riders during the pandemic. I kept getting better and better at making art on such a rough surface. I was also getting more customers when the pandemic was over. It now has become a business instead of a side hustle that I had in high school and I'm so gratful. Our vision is to send art to skaters all around the country who share a love for skateboarding that is equal to our company. If there are any problems with your purchase email us as soon as possible and we will help to the best of our abilities.

Our Prices

Our prices depend on the level of difficulty the piece will take to make. The griptape price is included in your purchase so when you pick out the kind of grip material you want that adds on approximately $10.00. If you've chosen a print or pattern we do those for $25.00. If you've chosen simple characters or word art thats between $35-$65 dollars. More detailed pieces are between $80-$120. The prices also vary on the amount of hours it takes to create the art piece that's why certain prices differ a bit.
